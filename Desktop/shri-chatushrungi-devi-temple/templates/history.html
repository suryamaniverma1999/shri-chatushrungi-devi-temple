About Maa Chatushrungi Devi

Maa Chatushrungi Devi is worshipped as a divine form of Goddess Durga. Devotees visit the temple with devotion and faith to seek blessings, strength and peace.

Shri Chatushrungi Devi Temple Trust works for daily worship, aarti, festival arrangements, devotee facilities, cleanliness, temple development and religious activities.

Faith and Devotion

The temple is a sacred center of devotion for devotees of Maa Chatushrungi Devi.

Daily Worship

Daily pooja, darshan and aarti are performed with devotion and discipline.

Festival Celebration

Navratri and other Hindu festivals are celebrated with devotion and enthusiasm.

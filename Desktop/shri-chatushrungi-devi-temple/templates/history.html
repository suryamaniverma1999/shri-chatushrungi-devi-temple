About Maa Chatushrungi Devi

Maa Chatushrungi Devi is worshipped as a powerful form of Goddess Durga. The temple is a sacred place where devotees come with faith, devotion and prayers.

The temple trust works for daily worship, festival arrangements, devotee facilities, cleanliness, maintenance and religious activities.

Temple Faith

The temple has been a center of devotion for devotees seeking blessings of Maa.

Daily Worship

Daily pooja, darshan and aarti are performed with devotion.

Festival Celebration

Navratri and other Hindu festivals are celebrated with great devotion.
